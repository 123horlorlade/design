About Salaudeen Kehinde Abdulsalam
Welcome to HORLORLADE DESIGN, where creativity meets passion and dedication. I am Salaudeen Kehinde Abdulsalam, the founder and driving force behind this venture.

The Genesis of HORLORLADE DESIGN

In 2022, at the young age of 17, I embarked on an incredible journey to establish this company in the vibrant heart of Nigeria. My inspiration was rooted in a vision that I nurtured while completing my 12th Grade. Backed by the unwavering support of my twin brother, Salaudeen Taiwo Salaudeen, and other beloved family members, HORLORLADE DESIGN came into being.

A Journey into Graphics Artistry

From an early age, my love for graphics artistry ignited. I began honing my skills at the tender age of 5 and evolved into a Graphics Pro known as "Graphics Premium" by the time I turned 16. The world of graphics became my canvas, and I was determined to paint it with creativity and innovation.

A Pursuit of Knowledge

My academic journey led me through various educational institutions. I started my science education at Ilorin Grammar School during my 7th Grade, continuing until the 11th Grade. The pursuit of knowledge then led me to Muslim Secondary Commercial School in Igbaja, where I completed my 12th Grade. There, I undertook the West African Examination Council (WAEC) and the National Examination Council (NECO) examinations, marking important milestones in my educational journey.

Professional Training

Upon the successful completion of my exams, I decided to embark on a path that would further enhance my skills. My professional journey started at Happy Printing Enterprises in Ilorin, Kwara State. Under the guidance of Mr. Lambert Kayode, my skills in graphics artistry were refined and honed. At the time, Happy Printing Enterprises was owned by the esteemed Mr. AdeBola Ezikel, who was once the Magaji of the Oko Erin Area in Ilorin, Kwara State.

Roots in Faith and Family

My educational foundation was laid at Muslim Faithfuls Academy (MUFCAD) Nursery and Primary School in Ilorin, Kwara State. This esteemed institution was founded by my parents, Mr. and Mrs. Salaudeen. The values instilled by my family and the nurturing environment at MUFCAD shaped my character and ambition. I invite you to explore the world of HORLORLADE DESIGN, where creativity knows no bounds, and innovation is our guiding star. Join me on this exciting journey, where every project is a canvas, and every idea comes to life in vibrant, artistic splendor.

Thank you for being a part of this journey with me.
